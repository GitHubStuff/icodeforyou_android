# 24.4.16 — `CarsViewModel` (GREEN)

## Objective

- Resolve the 24.4.15 red gate. One file.
- Byte-parallel to `CatsViewModel` (24.3.37 baseline): the query pipeline — debounce then `flatMapLatest` into the repository's single observable — is the ViewModel's whole job. Rapid typing subscribes only the final query; keystrokes never hammer FTS.

## Design notes

- `manufacturers` = `query` → `debounce(QUERY_DEBOUNCE_MILLIS)` → `flatMapLatest { carsRepository.manufacturers(it) }` → `stateIn(WhileSubscribed(SUBSCRIPTION_STOP_TIMEOUT_MS), emptyList())`.
- Refresh failures are signaled, never presented — the screen owns the 750 ms read-only toast.
- `isRefreshing` flips in try/finally: clears on success, failure, and cancellation alike. `CancellationException` rethrown, never swallowed.
- Koin registration (`viewModelOf(::CarsViewModel)`) is deliberately NOT here — it folds into the `CarsModule` reissue when `CarsScreen` lands and actually resolves it.
