## Design notes

- `carsModule` mirrors `catsModule` structurally, with two deliberate differences:
  - **No interceptor, no `newBuilder()`** — vPIC is keyless, so the shared `OkHttpClient` from `networkModule` is used as-is.
  - **No viewModel registrations yet** — `CarsViewModel` lands later in Phase 4; `viewModelOf(::CarsViewModel)` appends then via a reissue.
- Consumers inject domain contracts only (`CarsRepository`, `CarsRemoteSource`); Retrofit, Room, and wire types stay behind the module (DIP).
- `DatabaseModule` keeps its rule: features inject exactly the DAO they own — nothing outside `db/` asks Koin for the whole database.
- `AppModules` append order follows the phase convention: `carsModule` after `catsModule`.
