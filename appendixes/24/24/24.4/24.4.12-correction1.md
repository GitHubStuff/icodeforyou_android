# 24.4.12-correction1 — `CarApi` gains `BASE_URL`

## Root cause

- The house pattern (per `catsModule` → `CatApi.BASE_URL`) is that each Retrofit interface owns its base URL as a companion constant.
- 24.4.12 shipped `CarApi` without one, so `carsModule` (24.4.14) would have had nowhere sanctioned to read `https://vpic.nhtsa.dot.gov/api/` from.
- Fix: companion `BASE_URL` added. No other change.
