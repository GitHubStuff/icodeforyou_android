## Design

- **ISP, applied.** The upcoming `CoinsViewModel` (and later the Database
  Viewer's COINS refresh, §19) needs exactly one engine capability:
  `refresh()`. Depending on the concrete engine would drag `start()` —
  a bootstrap-only concern — into UI reach and force tests to mock a
  concrete class. One method, one interface.
- Next step reissues `CoinPollingEngine` implementing it — a declaration
  change only, `refresh()` already has the right signature.
